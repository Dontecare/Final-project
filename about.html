My name is LeDonte Delvaughn Carter. I am 23 years old and currently in college to expand my knowledge of art and creation.

My art is a journey through the lens of perception, a visual exploration of the multifaceted way in which we all experience the world. Inspired by the desire to unveil the unique beauty in our shared reality, I aim to challenge the notion that we all see things in the same light.

Within my artworks, I revel in the complexity and depth that may escape notice at first glance. Much like the subtleties of human perception, my creations reveal themselves gradually, encouraging viewers to delve beyond the surface and discover the intricate details that were previously overlooked.

Rooted in a profound appreciation for Psychology and Philosophy, I embrace doubt as a catalyst for introspection. Drawing inspiration from Descartes' assertion, "I Doubt, Therefore I Think, Therefore I Am," I invite viewers to question the depths of my work, fostering contemplation and, in turn, recognizing the unique essence within each piece.

In the realm of digital art, I strive to blend accessibility with nuance. Crafting projects that are easily digestible on the surface, I embed subtle details and Easter eggs, encouraging viewers to engage more deeply and unravel layers of meaning.

While proficient in various mediums, my primary focus is on Digital Art, employing an Abstract Realism style. This approach allows me to mirror the intricate dance of shapes, shades, and hues that form our perceptions. By presenting images with freeform shapes, devoid of lines or dots, I challenge the conventional ways we recognize objects, prompting contemplation on how our minds construct familiar concepts.

Delving into the realms of the human body and mind, my artworks aim to unravel the complexities that exist within the seemingly simple. Each stroke and hue contributes to a narrative that celebrates the intricate nature of individuals, echoing the profound truth that people, much like my art, are beautifully complex.

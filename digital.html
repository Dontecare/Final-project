Digital Art Process

Each piece I create is deeply influenced by my personal journey as an artist. I draw inspiration from the ongoing challenge of being truly understood, as too often people judge solely on appearances. Understanding the distinct perspective through which I view the world, I embark on a creative exploration to reveal the layers hidden beneath those first impressions.

To bring this complex inspiration into tangible form, I utilize a technique that reflects the subtleties of my perception. Starting with familiar visuals, I break down the world into shapes and patterns. Each component serves as a foundation, enabling me to capture the intricacies of my visual journey, one shape at a time, one pattern at a time.

The actual creation takes place in the digital realm, where Adobe Illustrator serves as my primary tool for drawing, and Adobe Photoshop becomes the medium for arranging the pieces. Working digitally allows me to bring my conceptual ideas to life seamlessly. With careful placement of abstract shapes, I weave together a visual story that embodies the essence of my individual viewpoint.

Bringing each piece together, I take a moment for reflection, diving into a thorough examination of the artwork. I ponder over potential nuances, considering how to amplify the intended message. This phase becomes a refining process, where I meticulously fine-tune elements to harmonize seamlessly with my vision, ensuring the artwork resonates with the intricate layers of my perspective

The completion brings a sense of fulfillment, as the artwork stands as a testament to the unique way I navigate and interpret the world, inviting others to perceive its depths with fresh eyes.
